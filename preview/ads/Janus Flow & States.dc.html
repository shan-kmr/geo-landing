F3 · THE DIVE — SHADE ON, HOVER CONTRACT z 12.6 · shade = population · tooltip follows cursor

NOTES
The hover contract: pointer cursor, brightened cell, and a tooltip that states the count, names the denominator, and always closes with click to open. Same four words at every res — a promise the click always keeps.
Data is ink density, never hue. Population shades white → graphite on a log ramp; the halftone gets finer as tiles split. A coarse-tile click flies exactly one ladder step down (950 ms) — zoom is never dumped on the user.
Emotional beat: gravity. The city pulls. Each split raises the stakes — the same gesture, finer truth.
