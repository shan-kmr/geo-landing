THE SPOT
Three real apps, one per move. One layer under all of them.
Same person moving through the city. At each move the phone shows a DIFFERENT real app doing its real location behavior — each a mirror of an app category people know. Movement shows in the transitions: the phone shrinks and rides the flying map between moves, then grows so each app reads clearly.
THE THREE MIRRORS (grounded in research)
Move 1 · Store Mode — walk into a store, the app becomes the store (scan, aisle map, in-stock here, this-store deals, loyalty). Mirrors Target / Walmart / DICK's. The only true live-geofence behavior; Radar's flagship (DICK's: 2.5× conversion).
Move 2 · Delivery storefront — get home, a different set of restaurants ranked by ETA/distance. Mirrors DoorDash / Uber Eats / Instacart.
Move 3 · Local discovery — head out, what's trending right here. Mirrors Yelp / TikTok Local Feed / Snap Map.
